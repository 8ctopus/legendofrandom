I'm not sure about your problem. Here's my short solution:

1. search for the good boy and the bad boy messages with "search for --> all referenced text strings", and you will find this:

11.jpg (113.93 KiB) Viewed 907 times


You see there are 2 bad boys, but we're interested in good boy, so putting cursor on 1st line of the good boy function tells you it's called from 0040124c.

Going there you see THE compare function, the CALL to good boy and the CALL to bad boy.

22.jpg (44.41 KiB) Viewed 907 times


So set a BP at line 001243 which is the line to patch (quick and dirty method), e.g. replace JE with JMP.
